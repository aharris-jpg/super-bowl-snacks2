America’s Favorite Super Bowl Snack — by State

Top snack in each state (survey results).
Methodology: We selected the most-chosen snack in each state from six featured options (Wings, Pizza, Chips & Dip, Nachos, Cheese Board, Potato Skins). “Other” snacks were not included.
Ties: If two snacks tied in a state, we used the national result from the “Total” column to break the tie.
Source: Survey conducted by RotoGrinders.com (n=3,008 U.S. adults).
